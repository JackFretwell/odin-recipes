Chicken and Halloumi Wraps

Description

A nice easy summer recipe that not only tastes great, but also doesn't need a ton of ingredients!

Ingredients

  • 2 mini chicken breast fillets
  • 1 halloumi cheese
  • 2 wraps of your choice
  • Olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons chilli flakes
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Â½ bag of iceburg lettuce
  • 1 tablespoon light mayo
  • 1 tablespoon sweet chilli sauce

Steps

  1. Place your chicken in a bowl and spray liberally with cooking spray (or drizzle over olive oil). Sprinkle on a tablespoon of oregano, a pinch of salt and pepper and a good pinch of chilli flakes. Cover and leave in the fridge for 30 minutes.
  2. BAke the chicken in the oven for 20 minutes at 180 degrees C.
  3. While the chicken bakes cut the halloumi into small (1-2cm) strips and fry in a little cooking oil or spray over medium heat, for about 4 minutes on each side, until golden brown.
  4. Spread wraps with the mayo and sweet chilli sauce (or other sauce), add salad, 2 mini chicken breast fillets and 3-4 strips of halloumi.
  5. Roll, either with both ends tucked in or an open end.
